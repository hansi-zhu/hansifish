3.

On the day of its grand opening, I woke up at 7:00 in the morning and hurried over.



Many people had already entered. There was evidence of a line having been formed hours before, although now the doorway was clear and open.

So you know me. I charged inside.

A man with a very large camera turned toward me. I smiled. He snapped a picture.

“Maybe I will make it onto the news,” I thought.

Fact: I did not.
